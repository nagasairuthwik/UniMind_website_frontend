Frequently Asked Questions

How does AI personalization work?

Our AI learns from your patterns and preferences to give you tailored recommendations across health, finance, productivity, and lifestyle.

Is my data secure?

Yes, all data is encrypted end-to-end and we never share it with third parties.

Can I export my data?

You can export reports anytime from Settings → Privacy & Security → Download My Data.

How to set custom goals?

Navigate to the Goal Setting screen from Dashboard or Insights, then add or edit your goals per domain.
